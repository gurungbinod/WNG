It is important to eat well and exercise to stay healthy. Almost everyday, new research confirms the role of nutrition in fighting disease and aging. At Washington Nutrition Group Nutrition, your dietitian nutritionist knows the benefits of different foods and nutrients, and keeps up with all the latest information about their benefits and applications to your specific health concerns and needs.

Each person is unique and has a slightly different metabolism rate. It is common for Washington Nutrition Group patients to believe their metabolism is slow or fast and the cause of excess weight gain or loss. To help diagnosis the situation, Washington Nutrition Group uses a state of the art metabolism test to measure your resting metabolic rate (RMR) which is the amount of calories your body requires each day to maintain minimum function (i.e. without exercise or extra activity).

In addition to metabolism testing, Washington Nutrition Group also utilizes several other tests to isolate other nutrition conditions such as:

  • Vitamin and Mineral Deficiencies
  • Antioxidant Levels
  • Food Sensitivities and Intolerances
  • Cholesterol Levels
  • Kidney and Liver Function
  • Blood Sugar Status

Personalized Fitness and Nutrition Programs
