Insurance & Billing

We are a preferred provider with most major insurance companies. Please note the type of nutrition coverage you have will depend on your specific plan contracted by your employer. To determine if nutrition services are covered, it is your responsibility to contact your insurance company. If you do not have nutrition benefits, ask your company's benefits department to cover medical nutrition therapy.

Benefits may be defined but not limited to, deductibles, co-pays, and co-insurance. Please call us if you have more questions about your benefits and our staff will direct you.
