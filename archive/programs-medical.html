Our weight loss program is for patients that need to lose weight as quickly and safely as possible. Clinically proven by Johns Hopkins University, our weight loss program has helped our patients lose more weight than other weight loss methods they have tried in the past, and has helped many become free of their blood pressure and adult onset diabetes medications. Our females typically lose 13-15 pounds a month on the program and our males typically lose 15-20 pounds a month on our program. Our program is also very affordable, and our patients see immediate results without hunger.

Our program is a combination of low calorie, low fat nutritionally balanced meal replacement products and real food with nutrition counseling with a licensed dietitian nutritionist provided to meet your individualized health and weight loss needs. There are three easy to follow stages to help you lose the weight quickly and safely and keep it off; the weight loss phase, transition phase and weight maintenance phase. There is no counting calories, carbohydrates or points. It is all done for you.


I have tried diets before. What is so different about your weight loss program?
We take the worry out of portion control and temptation by using balanced meals. You will be eating six times a day and will not feel deprived. We will provide you with the support, guidance, skills and other tools you need for long-term success. You also lose weight quicker than other programs which gives you the motivation to continue, and then transition to maintain your new weight. The meal plan typically consists of 5 meals a day, delivered to your door and 1 meat and vegetable meal prepared at home or in a restaurant. A vegetarian program is also available. When you become 5-10 pounds from your goal weight, we will teach you how to transition back to real foods and maintain your weight loss for life.

Who can benefit from this program?
If you are struggling with being overweight, obesity, diabetes, high blood pressure or joint pain or if you have tried every weight loss program or method out there without success or the last 10-20 pounds will just not budge this program is designed for you.

How do I get started?
Contact us to schedule a medical weight loss consultation.



Program Options

Option #1: In Office: Meet with one our licensed dietitian nutritionists at one of our office locations.

Our dietitian nutritionists will help you determine if you are a good candidate for this program, take your weight and BMI, and help you determine a healthy weight goal and the amount of weight you will lose with this program. We will also explain the program protocols and spend time addressing your questions and concerns. All sessions are performed in a professional one-on-one medical office setting. One of our health coaches will also contact you by phone or email weekly. There is no charge for phone and email exchanges with one of our dietitian nutritionists while you are on the weight management program.

Please contact us to find out if your insurance plan covers this nutrition visit.


Option #2: Over the Phone: One of our licensed dietitian nutritionists will explain the program and help you get started over the phone at no charge.

Please contact us the first day you start the program so we can help make sure you are on the right track. One of our dietitian nutritionists is available by phone and email to answer any questions you have while on the weight management program. One of our health coaches will also check in with you weekly to help you stay accountable. There is no cost for phone and email exchanges with our health coaches while you are on the weight management program.

Please contact us to find out if your insurance plan covers this nutrition visit.
