Clinical nutrition counseling for all medical conditions including but not limited to: Bariatric Surgery, Diabetes Type I and II, High Blood Pressure, High Cholesterol, Fertility, PCOS, Food Allergy, Gout, Gerd, other Gastrointestional related conditions, and Eating disorders.		   Weight Loss Seminars and Support Groups
