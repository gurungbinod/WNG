Traditional Weight loss is for children and adults who want to lose weight with or without medical conditions including but not limited to :Bariatric Surgery Nutrition, Diabetes Type I and II, High Blood Pressure, High Cholesterol, Fertility, PCOS, Food Allergy, Gout, Gerd, other Gastrointestional related conditions, that need to lose weight. At your first nutrition session, a registered dietitian will meet with you for a nutrition assessment. We will then most likely recommend follow-up nutrition sessions.

What is covered during traditional weight loss sessions?

Nutrition Assessment
During a 60 minute nutrition assessment our nutritionist will ask you a variety of health questions, assess your current dietary patterns, and will help you design a nutrition program including the amount of calories, fat, protein, carbohydrates, vitamins and minerals you need to reach your weight and health goals. To determine your most accurate caloric number, please let us know if you are interested in metabolism testing when you schedule your appointment.

Follow-up nutrition sessions
During a 30 minute follow-up session our dietitian nutritionist will perform a dietary assessment and discuss the progress towards your health goals.

What is the Difference Between Nutrition Counseling, Traditional Weight Loss, and Medical Weight Loss?

Nutrition Counseling
Nutrition counseling is for children and adults with medical conditions including but not limited to:Bariatric Surgery, Diabetes Type I and II, High Blood Pressure, High Cholesterol, Fertility, PCOS, Food Allergy, Gout, Gerd, other Gastrointestional related conditions, and Eating disorders. At your first nutrition session, a registered dietitian will meet with you for a nutrition assessment. We will then most likely recommend follow-up nutrition sessions.

Traditional Weight Loss
If you do not mind losing 1-2 pounds a week, or have less than 10 pounds to lose, have never met with a nutrition professional or have never tried other programs we recommend traditional weight loss. At your first nutrition session one of our dietitian nutritionists will meet with you for a nutrition assessment. We do recommend metabolism testing, so we can accurately determine how many calories you need at this first assessment.

Medical Weight Loss Program
If you want to lose weight fast and safe, have more than 30 pounds to lose and have weight related medical conditions such as; diabetes and/or high blood pressure we will most likely recommend our weight loss program because we want you to get the weight off as soon as possible and help you maintain your new weights. Our Take Shape for Life Weight Loss Program will help you lose more weight and get off your medications in a shorter period of time with out sacrificing your health than traditional weight loss.
