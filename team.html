Our Team

Our Team of Registered Dietitians, Certified Personal Trainers and Certified Health Coaches look forward to helping you reach your health goals.

DIRECTOR OF WASHGINTON NUTRITION GROUP
Christine Haas MS, LDN, CNS, CPT
Christine Haas M.S., L.D.N., C.N.S., C.P.T. is the president and director of Washington Nutrition Group Nutrition and Personal Training. Christine is also a licensed nutritionist in Maryland and Washington DC certified through the American College of Nutrition and is a personal trainer certified through the National Academy of Sports Medicine. Christine implements and oversees all programs at Washington Nutrition Group and manages the medical weight loss program. Christine now spends a majority of her time helping doctors incorporate nutrition and weight management programs into their practices.

Christine is a member of the American Dietetic Association and holds a number of fitness and nutrition certifications, including The Certificate of Training in Adult Weight Management from the American Dietetic Association. Christine has also received a Masters of Science Degree in Health and Fitness Management from The American University in Washington D.C. and a Masters Degree in Nutrition Education from The University of Vermont.

Christine worked as the nutritionist and as a personal trainer at The Sports Club L.A. in Washington D.C. as a nutritionist for Medifast at corporate headquarters in Owings Mills, MD and as an adjunct professor of nutrition at Montgomery College. Christine also manages all nutrition and fitness related activities at
the Center of Obesity Surgery in Washington DC, and at the Obesity
Surgery Center in Herndon, VA and is a regular nutrition and fitness
correspondent for Northern Virginia Magazine.

  
 

REGISTERED DIETITIANS
Kelly McGuffin, MS, RD
Registered Dietitian

Kelly McGuffin, M.S., R.D. is a registered dietitian with 12 years of experience as a registered dietitian and a certified health coach with Medifast/Take Shape for Life with a bachelor degree in dietetics and a minor in public health/health assessment and promotion from James Madison University. Kelly graduated Magna Cum Laude and went on to complete her dietetic internship from the University of Virginia. Kelly also received her master's degree in nutrition from Central Michigan University where she graduated Summa Cum Laude. Kelly has a diverse background working in the hospital setting as well as the outpatient setting. Areas of practice include working with both the pediatric and adult population with obesity and a diverse range of medical conditions from celiac disease, diabetes, bariatric surgery, heart disease, eating disorders, kidney disease, pulmonary disease, substance abuse, and much more. Kelly is passionate with helping clients meet their nutritional needs and reaching their goals.

Locations: McLean and Ashburn		  

 

Mary Warner, RD, LDN
Registered Dietitian

Mary Warner, R.D., L.D.N., is a registered dietitian who recently relocated to Northern Virginia from Greenville, North Carolina. Mary has 8 years of experience as a registered dietitian and is a member of the American Dietetic Association. Mary graduated Summa Cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science degree in Nutrition and Dietetics and also completed her dietetic internship program at East Carolina University. This experience has given her knowledge in regards to sports nutrition and adequate eating habits for athletes.

Genevieve Clarke, RDN, LDN, CDE
Registered Dietitian

Genevieve Clarke, R.D.N., L.D.N., C.D.E. is a registered dietitian and certified diabetes educator licensed in Maryland and the District of Columbia and is a member of both the American Dietetic Association and American Association of Diabetes Educators . Genevieve has a broad range of experience from both inpatient and outpatient hospital settings to physician supervised weight loss programs. Genevieve served as the outpatient dietitian for the diabetes management program at MedStar Southern Maryland Hospital Center and as the dietitian at a well know local physician supervised weight loss clinic. At MedStar through nutrition skill building, weight loss, lifestyle change and insulin management Genevieve helped clients control their diabetes. At MedStar Genevieve also coached patients in a chronic disease prevention program designed to help patients lose weight and decrease their high cholesterol, high blood pressure and/or pre-diabetes through nutrition and lifestyle changes in order to reduce their risk of heart attack, stroke and diabetes.

Genevieve has a Bachelor of Science degree in Food Science with a concentration in Human Nutrition at Clemson University. Genevieve completed her dietetic internship through Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University. During her internship Genevieve performed her medical nutrition therapy rotation at Inova Fairfax Hospital and completed specialty training at bariatric surgery centers, weight management programs and sports nutrition sites throughout Northern Virginia. Genevieve also holds the Group Fitness Instructor Certification through the American Council on Exercise and is a practicing group exercise instructor and personal trainer.

As a dietitian, Genevieve takes a balanced approach to nutrition. With extensive experience in nutrition counseling for weight management, cholesterol improvement, diabetes control, high blood pressure, insulin training, binge eating disorder, bariatric surgery, general wellness and various gastrointestinal issues her goal is to make sure every client leaves her office with the knowledge they need to improve their health significantly.

Locations: Washington DC

Claudia Booker BS, CPT - Personal Trainer

Claudia Booker, B.S., C.P.T., is a certified personal trainer with numerous fitness and personal training certifications. When Claudia was nine she lost her father to a fatal heart attack creating emotional issues that lead to a severe eating disorder. Today Claudia has a very successful and passionate career in health and fitness. Claudia knows what it is like to struggle with feeling good and being healthy and to overcome obstacles to become fit and strong, both mentally and physically. Claudia has learned to nourish her body, not abuse it and guides her clients through their journey towards fitness and wellness with understanding, education and support. Her approach is always overall health; a healthy diet, healthy exercise and a healthy emotional outlook. Claudia has experience in many environments including corporate, private, and commercial. She has worked with a populations of all ages, men and women and fitness levels ranging from athletes to special needs. Claudia always says that it's called "Personal" training for a reason. She believes every client is different, and their program should be designed personally and specifically for them.

Locations: McLean, Herndon, Oakton, Reston, Fairfax
